React First Anomaly Containment

Class C

RFAC-001: The Smiling Man

A figure with an unnaturally wide grin who appears in peripheral vision. Prolonged exposure causes intense paranoia and hallucinations.

RFAC-002: The Shapeshifter

An entity that can change its form to mimic other beings, making it difficult to identify and predict.

RFAC-003: The Whispering Shadow

A shadowy figure that whispers unsettling secrets into the ears of its victims, driving them to madness.

RFAC-004: The Haunting Melody

A haunting melody that causes extreme emotional distress and aggression in those who hear it repeatedly.

RFAC-005: The Living Wall

A wall that absorbs living beings into its surface, leaving them trapped and visible as trapped shadows.

RFAC-006: The Phantom Voice

An entity that can mimic any voice and use it to manipulate or deceive others.

RFAC-007: The Wraith

A spectral being that induces fear and despair in those it encounters, appearing as a misty figure.

RFAC-008: The Glitch

An anomaly that disrupts electronic devices, causing them to malfunction in unpredictable ways.

RFAC-009: The Silent Room

A room where no sound can be heard, causing extreme psychological stress and disorientation.

RFAC-010: The Unseen Hand

A presence that moves objects without being visible, creating a sense of being watched and manipulated.

Class B

RFAC-011: The Gorgon

A creature that petrifies those who make eye contact with it, turning them into stone statues.

RFAC-012: The Inkblot

A shifting inkblot that induces hallucinations and paranoia in those who stare at it for too long.

RFAC-013: The Timekeeper

An entity that manipulates time within its vicinity, causing time distortions and sudden temporal jumps.

RFAC-014: The Mirror's Edge

A mirror that reflects not only physical appearances but also the darkest fears and desires of those who look into it.

RFAC-015A: The Feral Howler

A beast with a primal howl that causes severe disorientation and physical pain to those who hear it, though it is less intense than its counterpart.

RFAC-016: The Abyssal Gaze

An entity with eyes that induce deep existential dread and despair in those who meet its gaze.

RFAC-017: The Contagion

A being that spreads a mysterious illness causing severe physical and mental deterioration.

RFAC-018: The Tremor

An anomaly that causes localized earthquakes and ground instability, creating hazardous environments.

RFAC-019: The Beacon

A light source that attracts dangerous entities and causes anxiety in those exposed to its glow.

RFAC-020: The Ectoplasmic Entity

A being made of ectoplasm that can pass through solid objects and causes severe psychic disturbances.

Class A

RFAC-021: The Mutilator

A being that causes extreme physical mutilation to those it touches, leaving them permanently scarred.

RFAC-022: The Void

An area that exists outside normal space and time, inducing hallucinations and reality distortions.

RFAC-023: The Faceless One

A creature with no face that induces intense psychological trauma and loss of identity.

RFAC-024: The Devourer

A creature that feeds on the life force of other beings, leaving them as empty husks.

RFAC-025: The Enigma

A puzzling entity that confounds and confuses those around it with its unpredictable behavior.

RFAC-026: The Leviathan

A colossal sea creature that emerges from the depths to wreak havoc on coastal areas.

RFAC-027: The Transmuter

A being that can alter the physical properties of objects and living beings, causing unpredictable mutations.

RFAC-028: The Tempest

An entity that generates violent weather phenomena, including storms and tornadoes.

RFAC-029: The Aberrant

A being that defies natural laws, creating unpredictable and often dangerous phenomena.

Class S

RFAC-037: The Oblivion

A destructive force that erases all matter and energy within its vicinity, leaving nothing but emptiness behind.

RFAC-032: The Infinite Maze

A labyrinthine anomaly that continuously shifts and reconfigures itself, trapping anyone who enters in a never-ending cycle of disorientation and terror.

RFAC-031: The Eternal Flame

A flame that burns perpetually and can incinerate anything it touches, including intangible entities and phenomena.

RFAC-034: The Phantom King

A spectral monarch who commands legions of wraith-like entities, capable of manipulating reality within its domain.

RFAC-033: The Singularity

A gravitational anomaly that warps space-time, causing intense gravitational forces and the collapse of nearby structures.

RFAC-035: The Voidwalker

An entity that traverses between dimensions, causing rifts that can pull objects and people into alternate realities or unknown dangers.

RFAC-036: The Nightmarer

A being that induces vivid and horrifying nightmares that manifest into reality, tormenting its victims both mentally and physically.

RFAC-038: The Cosmic Dreadnought

An enormous entity that wields cosmic powers, causing widespread destruction and chaos wherever it appears.

RFAC-030: The Apex Predator

A relentless creature with unparalleled hunting abilities and extreme physical strength, capable of overpowering any adversary with ease.

RFAC-015B: The Howler

An entity that emits an extraordinarily powerful and deafening howl capable of inducing extreme disorientation, physical pain, and severe psychological trauma to those who hear it. Its effects can incapacitate entire groups and cause lasting damage.
